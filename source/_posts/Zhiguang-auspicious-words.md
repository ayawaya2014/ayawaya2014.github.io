We must do the right karma

that conforms to the way of sages

and does not harm all beings,

and it is best to benefit all beings.

This is always the most important principle.

<!--more -->

### 智廣吉祥心語

一定要做

符合聖賢之道

不傷害衆生

最好還能夠

利益衆生的正業

這一條始終都是

最重要的原則
